CatDog

About

CatDog is a creature that is part cat and part dog. It appeals to both cat and dog individuals by having the hostile elements of cats and the likeness 'best friend' qualities of a dog.

CatDog is anatomically impossible, yet Gen Z'ers fell in love with the mysterious exsistence of such a creature.

Things that must be remembered:

  1. CatDog is two souls trapped in one body, lacking tail and hind legs.
  2. Cat is sophisiticated and cultured, while Dog is fun-loving and capricious.
  3. CatDog have no offspring and, are therefore, one-of-a-kind.
